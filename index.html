How to Play

Jeopardy BJJ Edition

🎯 Goal

Two teams compete to earn the most points by correctly answering BJJ trivia. The board has 5 categories with 5 clues each, worth $200 to $1,000. Highest score wins.

📋 Selecting a Clue

Tap any dollar amount on the board to reveal that clue. The host reads it aloud, then taps Show Answer to reveal the correct answer.

✅ Scoring a Clue

After the answer is shown, tap the correct button for whichever team answered:

  • ✓ +$X — Team answered correctly. Their score goes up.
  • ✗ -$X — Team answered incorrectly. Their score goes down.
  • No Score — Close — Nobody answered, or the host decides not to award points. Score unchanged.

If nobody has revealed the answer yet, the footer shows Skip — no score to cancel the clue without revealing it.

⭐ Daily Double

Two clues per board are hidden Daily Doubles. When revealed, the answering team chooses a wager (minimum $200, maximum their current score). The wager is added or deducted based on whether they get it right.

🏆 Final Jeopardy

Tap Final Jeopardy in the footer at any time (usually after the board is complete). Both teams secretly enter a wager, then the clue is revealed. The host awards or deducts each team's wager individually based on their answer.

🔄 Rounds & Next Round

Once all 25 clues on the board are used, a Next Round → button appears. Tap it to load a fresh board with new questions. The game includes 300 unique clues — enough for 10+ complete rounds.

🔧 Manual Score Controls

The small − and + buttons flanking each team's score adjust it by $100 per tap. Use these for corrections or tie-breaking adjustments outside of normal clue scoring.

Double-tap a team name to rename it.
